Clodovis Boff

b. 1944 · Brazil

Clodovis Boff, OSM (b. 1944, Brazil), Brazilian Servite priest and systematic theologian, a co-founder of liberation theology's method who co-wrote 'Introducing Liberation Theology' with his brother Leonardo and authored 'Theology and Praxis'. From 2007 he publicly broke with the current's method, arguing it had put the poor in the place of Christ; he has taught at PUC-Rio and PUC-Paraná.[1] [2] [3]
