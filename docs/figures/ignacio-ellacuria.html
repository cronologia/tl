Ignacio Ellacuría

1930–1989 · El Salvador

Ignacio Ellacuría (1930–1989), Basque-born Jesuit philosopher and theologian in El Salvador and a disciple of Xavier Zubiri. He developed a 'philosophy of historical reality' and the method of 'historicization'. As rector of the UCA (from 1979) he pressed for a negotiated peace; he and seven others were killed by Salvadoran soldiers on 16 November 1989.[1] [2] [3] [4]
