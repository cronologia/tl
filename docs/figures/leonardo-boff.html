Leonardo Boff

b. 1938 · Brazil

Leonardo Boff (b. 1938, Concórdia, Brazil) is a Brazilian theologian and former Franciscan priest, a leading figure of Latin American liberation theology. Silenced by the Congregation for the Doctrine of the Faith over 'Church: Charism and Power' (1981), he left the priesthood and Franciscan order in 1992 and later turned to eco-theology, base ecclesial communities and integral ecology.[1] [2] [3]
