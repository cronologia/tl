John Paul II

1920–2005 · Vatican

Pope 1978–2005. Warned against political 're-readings' of Christ at Puebla (1979) and presided over the disciplinary era of the 1980s, while his 1986 letter to the Brazilian bishops also used affirming language — both sides cited.[1] [2]
