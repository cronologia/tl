Hugo Assmann

1933–2008 · Brazil

Hugo Assmann (1933–2008), Brazilian theologian and educator, one of the earliest and most radical methodologists of the current. Exiled during Brazil's military dictatorship, he helped found the Departamento Ecuménico de Investigaciones (DEI) in Costa Rica. He later left the priesthood, married, and turned to critiques of market 'idolatry' and to pedagogy.[1] [2] [3] [4]
