Pedro Casaldáliga

1928–2020 · Brazil

Spanish-born Claretian missionary and bishop-prelate of São Félix do Araguaia (1971–2005). A pastoral figure and poet claimed by the current rather than a systematic theologian, he championed Indigenous people and landless peasants amid violent Araguaia land conflicts, co-founded the Comissão Pastoral da Terra, and drew Vatican scrutiny for his activism.[1] [2] [3]
