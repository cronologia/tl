Alfonso López Trujillo

1935–2008 · Colombia

Colombian cardinal (1935–2008). Elected CELAM secretary-general at Sucre, Bolivia, on 22 November 1972 and CELAM president from 1979; widely described as the leading intra-Latin-American institutional opponent of liberation theology. Later president of the Pontifical Council for the Family; died in Rome, 19 April 2008.[1] [2] [3]
