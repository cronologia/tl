Leo XIV (Robert Prevost)

b. 1955 · USA / Peru / Vatican

Pope from May 2025; spent decades as a missionary and bishop in Peru. Liberation theologians' expressions of hope about his pontificate, and his 'Dilexi te' (2025), are reported with debate about their significance.[1] [2]
