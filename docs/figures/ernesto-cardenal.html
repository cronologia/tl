Ernesto Cardenal

1925–2020 · Nicaragua

Ernesto Cardenal (1925–2020), Nicaraguan poet and Catholic priest, a leading voice of the current in Central America. He founded the Solentiname contemplative-artistic community, whose Gospel dialogues became 'El Evangelio en Solentiname'. Sandinista Minister of Culture (1979–1987), suspended from ministry by John Paul II, he later broke with Daniel Ortega and had his suspension lifted by Pope Francis in 2019.[1] [2] [3]
